TANजाईन

TANजाईन is a fashion and lifestyle blog for young professionals where you can discover about the sustainable Indian brands.

Sustainable fashion (also known as eco-fashion) is a movement and process of fostering change to fashion products and the fashion system towards greater ecological integrity and social justice. Sustainable fashion concerns more than just addressing fashion textiles or products. We here at TANजाईन promote Indian Homegrown labels for apparel and accessories.
